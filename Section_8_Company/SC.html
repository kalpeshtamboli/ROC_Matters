Section 8 Company

If you are interested in starting a company designed around non-profit objectives – fields include environment, trade, commerce, social welfare, arts, sports research, charity, education, religion, and more – then a Section 8 Company will be a great choice. This type of company needs the involvement of at least two directors. There is no absolute need for minimum paid-up capital. India encourages the creation of such companies under the Registrar of societies or as a Non-profit company. This is covered under The Company Act, 2013. Profits from Section 8 Companies are used to further promote its core objectives and not merely distributed as dividends among shareholders. This framework sets it apart from other companies. We rocmatters offer everything you need by way of counsel and guidance on how to start and secure your own Section 8 company
